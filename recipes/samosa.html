Samosa

Ingridents

  • potato 4 pieces
  • Maida
  • green chilli
  • oil
  • black salt ,coriender leaf

STEPS

  1. Boil the Potatoes for 10 min
  2. Smash The boiled potatos after removing their outer covring
  3. heat oil in pan add chilly salt,other spices as you like then add smashed potatoes
  4. Take all the samosa filling in a boul after it is prepared
  5. Make omosa shells from maida and add filling to it
  6. fry the somosa in oil until they turn golden
