Basmati-rice

Ingridients

  • 1 ½ cups basmati rice
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 (2 inch) piece cinnamon stick
  • 2 pods green cardamom 2 whole cloves
  • 1 tablespoon cumin seed
  • 1 small onion, thinly sliced 2 ½ cups water 1 teaspoon salt, or to taste

    STEPS

  1. Place rice into a bowl with enough water to cover. Set aside to soak for 20 minutes.
  2. In the last 10 minutes of soaking, heat oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add cinnamon stick, and cloves, then stir.
  3. Add sliced onion
  4. fry onion till they turn golden
  5. Add rice to it after draining water
  6. fry for 5 min then add water and let it cook for 10 min
